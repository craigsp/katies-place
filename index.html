Right here, right now

A breath, lying down

About 2 minutes · no need to sit up

You don't have to move. Wherever you are is fine. Let your eyes close, or just let them go soft toward something still.

Find your breath without changing it. Just notice it arriving, and leaving.

Now let the next breath in be a little slower — in, two, three, four. And out, slower still — out, two, three, four, five, six.

If your mind wanders, that isn't a mistake. Just come back to the next breath. The queasiness, the noise, the fear — let it be here. You're only breathing alongside it.

A few more, your own pace. In, gently. Out, longer. When you're ready, let your breath find its own rhythm again. You did the whole thing. That counts.
